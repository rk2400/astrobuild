VEDIC ASTROLOGY

Indian Astrology, also known as Vedic Astrology or Jyotish Shastra, is an ancient and divine science that reveals the connection between human life and cosmic influences. Rooted in the sacred Vedas, it is based on the precise movements of planets, stars, and the Moon, offering deep insights into destiny, karma, and future possibilities.

SCIENCE OF ASTROLOGY

Unlike Western astrology, which focuses on the Sun sign, Indian astrology uses the Moon sign (Rashi) and Nakshatras (constellations) to provide highly accurate and personalized predictions. A person’s Janam Kundli (birth chart) is created using the date, time, and place of birth, mapping planetary positions that shape life events.

KEY ASPECTS OF INDIAN ASTROLOGY

  • 12 Zodiac Signs (Rashis): Represent personality and life patterns.
  • 9 Planets (Navagrahas): Influence different aspects of life.
  • 12 Houses (Bhavas): Indicate specific life areas like career, relationships, and health.
  • Dasha System: Planetary periods affecting life cycles.
  • Yogas & Doshas: Special planetary combinations that bring fortune or challenges.
